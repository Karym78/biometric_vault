RGPD

Privacy and consent

This demo applies the basic RGPD principles expected for biometric data handling.

Principle Project implementation
ConsentRegistration requires explicit consent before storing a biometric template.
MinimizationLogin uses hashes and watermarked copies instead of exposing the raw fingerprint as the credential.
SecurityFiles are encrypted with AES-256-GCM and biometric access is blocked after repeated failures.
AccountabilitySecurity actions are recorded in SQLite logs.
TransparencyThe dashboard shows where hashes, watermarked images and encrypted files are stored.
